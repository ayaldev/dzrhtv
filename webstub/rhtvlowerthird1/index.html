Malacañang does not object the Sandiganbayan’s decision to grant detained Senator Juan Ponce Enrile hospital arrest while the court hears his graft and plunder charges. “I don’t think there was any objection that was interposed in the proceedings,” Deputy Presidential Spokesperson Abigail Valte said Saturday. Valte said President Aquino considers Enrile’s age and health. Enrile is 90-years-old. “Thus far, there is no objection. As in the pork scam cases, we’ve always abided by the decisions of the handling court especially when it comes to detention,” she said. Enrile is under hospital detention at the Philippine National Police hospital in Camp Crame, Quezon City in connection with the pork barrel scam where he is accused of pocketing kickbacks from his congressional pork barrel.
