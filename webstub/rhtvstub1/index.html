Welcome

DZRH News Television is a 24/7 cable news and information channel of Manila Broadcasting Company, and the teleradyo of DZRH-AM that airs throughout Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao. Carried nationwide with over 1,000 cable operators in all parts of the Philippine archipelago, in partnership with FICAP (Federation of International Cable Association of the Philippines) with growing number of cable partners and PCTA (Philippine Cable TV Association).

More

MBC

Manila Broadcasting Company, known as the “Asia’s Largest Media Network”, with 300 stations nationwide in the likes of DZRH, Love Radio, Yes FM, Easy Rock, Aksyon Radyo and Radyo Natin; has gone it’s way up to a bigger avenue through television, creating DZRH News Television, formerly known as RHTV, with up-to-date and comprehensive program line-up in a very informative and trendy format.
